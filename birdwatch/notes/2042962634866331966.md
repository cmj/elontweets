#### MISLEADING

[[view note]](https://x.com/i/birdwatch/n/2042971598274154573) - poetic-lime-crane (Shown notes: 12 · Rating impact: 207)\
This claim is misleading. Research shows people convicted of homicide rarely commit another homicide after release. Studies consistently find repeat homicide rates are very low; reoffending, when it occurs, is usually for non-homicide crimes.
